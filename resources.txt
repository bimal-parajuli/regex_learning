Some really useful websites:

1.  https://regex101.com/
    - To test out the regular expressions along with the test strings.
    - It also displays the following and hence helps determine the efficiency of our regex: 
                        -Number of matches
                        -Number of steps needed to make the match.
                        -Time taken

 2. https://regexone.com/
    - Consists of 15 lessons to learn the concepts and apply immediately.
    - and 8 curated exercise questions exclusively to test the regex knowledge.
